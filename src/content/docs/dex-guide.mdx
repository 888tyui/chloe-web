# DEX Guide

CHLOE's DEX page provides token swapping on Solana via Jupiter V6 aggregation.

## How to Swap

1. Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom or Solflare)
2. Select the token you want to sell (From)
3. Select the token you want to buy (To)
4. Enter the amount
5. Review the route and price impact
6. Click Swap to execute

## Supported Tokens

- SOL, USDC, USDT
- RAY, BONK, JUP
- PYTH, WIF
- And many more via Jupiter

## Price Ticker

The scrolling ticker at the top shows live prices for major tokens, updated every 30 seconds.

## Liquidity Pools

The Pool tab is coming soon. It will allow you to provide liquidity to pools and earn trading fees.

## Safety Tips

- Always check the price impact before swapping
- Use reasonable slippage settings (default 0.5%)
- Never invest more than you can afford to lose
- Verify token addresses when trading new tokens
